About Me

I'm currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science at the University of Houston, where I'm specializing in cybersecurity and honing my programming skills every day. My true passion lies in front-end development—creating clean, responsive, and interactive interfaces that deliver meaningful user experiences.

Before diving into tech, I spent over seven years working as a professional photographer. That journey trained my eye for visual storytelling, design balance, and user-centric aesthetics—skills that now play a major role in how I approach web design and development.

With a unique combination of artistic sensitivity and technical knowledge, I strive to build websites that are not only visually striking but also secure, accessible, and reliable. Whether it's through thoughtful UI design or strong backend structure, I aim to bridge the gap between beauty and functionality in every project I take on.
